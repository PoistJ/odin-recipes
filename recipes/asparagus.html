Oven Roasted Asparagus

Description

A simple oven roasted asparagus recipe, great and easy for meal prep and pairs well with the oven baked trout.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of asparagus spears
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Celsius.
  2. Cut off hard end of asparagus, cut asparagus into desired lengths.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, toss asparagus in olive oil until evenly covered.
  4. Add seasonings and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Spread evenly on baking sheet(s) and bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Asparagus should be tender. Take out and serve.
