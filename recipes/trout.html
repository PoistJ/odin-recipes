Oven Baked Trout Recipe

Description

This is an easy recipe for oven baked trout with very simple ingredients. It's quick to make and a great option for healthy meal prep for the week.

Minimal tools are needed for this recipe. In fact, all that's needed is an oven tray, a cutting board, and some aluminum foil.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp minced garlic (or 2 cloves as 1 tsp = 1 clove)
  • 1 tsp Italian herb seasoning
  • 1 medium lemon

Note: Adjust ingredients proportionate to amount of salmon.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees, arrange salmon fillets on baking sheet(s). Add salt and pepper to season.
  2. While oven is preheating, mix together seasonings in small bowl.
  3. Spread seasoning evenly over the fillets. Use spoon if needed.
  4. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until salmon is opaque and flaky. Be careful not to overcook the salmon.
  5. Take out of the oven and serve with your choice of rice, salad, or sides.
